My First Website 19 January 2022

This is my first website that actually go live. It was a long story, back in 2019 when I bought a domain named haniffridzun.com and started learning HTML and CSS to develop my personal website. I was very excited, try to make it responsive to any screen size, design beautiful UI that will be a treat for the eyes. However, my website wasn't finished, not until this date.

Back then, while researching on the project, I was distracted. I was exposed to too many shiny techs: C#, Python, R, DART, Java, and more. Out of curiousity, I research deeper into their glorious paths, Game Dev, Data Analytics, Mobile Dev. They shine so bright that I made a leap, LOL. From one jump to another, I realized that I tried to learn too many things without sticking with a single path and did not really master it completely.

So here my take. Stay consistent with a single path that we have chosen. Be committed to practice every day and make progress to finish the project. Acknowledge each progress we made each day will boost our confidence and self-assurance in our abilities. Finally, live in the present moment.
